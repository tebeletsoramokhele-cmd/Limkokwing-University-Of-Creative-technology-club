Explore and Join Our Exciting Clubs

Drama Club

The Drama Club brings stories to life on stage. Members explore acting, directing, and stage design through plays, skits, and annual performances that inspire creativity and teamwork.

Tech Club

The Tech Club encourages innovation through coding, robotics, and web development. Students collaborate on real-world projects and attend workshops that expand their digital skills.

Debate Club

The Debate Club sharpens public speaking and critical thinking skills. Members engage in structured debates and friendly competitions, promoting confidence and persuasive communication.

Sports Club

The Sports Club keeps students active and healthy through various activities like football, athletics, and volleyball. It fosters teamwork, discipline, and school spirit.
